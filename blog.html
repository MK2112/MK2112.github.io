DreamerV2, Explained.

February 2024 - Reading Time: 6 Minutes

DreamerV2 is a reinforcement learning algorithm. It's main goal is to be applicable to sequential decision problems in various environments. To achieve this, DreamerV2 curiously introduces several key innovations aimed at improving both efficiency and effectiveness in solving complex control tasks through reinforcement learning.
